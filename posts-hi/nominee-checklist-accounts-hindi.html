Nominee Checklist: Bank, FD, Insurance aur Demat me kya check karein

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Bahut families ko baad me pata chalta hai ki nominee details add hi nahi thi, outdated thi, ya alag-alag accounts me mismatch thi. Isse paperwork aur stress dono badh jate hain. Nominee review ek simple financial housekeeping task hai jo future confusion ko kam kar sakta hai.

Quick Answer

Har saal aur major life change ke baad nominee details review karo. Savings account, FD, insurance, demat, aur mutual fund sab ko alag-alag check karna chahiye.

Kahaan nominee check karna hai

  • savings aur salary accounts
  • FDs aur recurring deposits
  • life insurance policies
  • demat aur trading accounts
  • mutual fund folios

Checklist Table

ItemKya verify karein
Name spellingIdentity records se match ho.
Relationship detailsClaim ya request ke waqt clarity deti hain.
Multiple productsEk update sab jagah apply nahi hota.
Record locationTrusted family member ko basic info pata ho.

Simple Annual Review

  1. Saare important accounts list karo.
  2. Check karo nominee added hai ya nahi.
  3. Current details confirm karo.
  4. Summary ek secure jagah store karo.
  5. Ek trusted family member ko record location batao.

Common Mistakes

  1. Sochna ki ek bank update sab products cover kar dega.
  2. Marriage ya family change ke baad nominee update na karna.
  3. Records sirf dimag me rakhna, written summary na banana.
  4. Old low-balance accounts ignore kar dena.
