Personal Loan Rejection Reasons: loan reject hone ke common reasons aur fix

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Loan rejection aksar random nahi hota. Usually profile me kuch clear weak points hote hain jaise high EMI pressure, weak credit behavior, unstable income, ya document mismatch. Achhi baat ye hai ki inme se kaafi cheezein improve ki ja sakti hain.

Quick Answer

Personal loan aksar high EMI burden, low credit score, unstable income, recent multiple applications, ya document mismatch ki wajah se reject hota hai. Sabse pehle weakest area identify karke usko fix karna better hota hai, baar-baar apply karna nahi.

Common Reasons

  1. high existing EMI pressure
  2. low ya damaged credit score
  3. bahut recent applications
  4. income pattern unstable hona
  5. job history weak hona
  6. document mismatch
  7. bank statement stress signals
  8. requested amount zyada aggressive hona

Kya Fix Karein

ProblemPractical fix
high EMI burdenAmount reduce karein ya cashflow improve karein.
credit issuePayments stable rakhein aur card stress kam karein.
too many applicationsPause karein aur repeat hard checks avoid karein.
document mismatchDetails correct karke hi reapply karein.
