Bank Fraud Complaint Steps: pehle 30 minute me kya karein

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Fraud situation me fast aur structured action sabse important hota hai. Account protect kijiye, evidence save kijiye, aur sirf official support channels use kijiye. Panic se zyada kaam checklist karti hai.

Quick Answer

Unauthorized transaction dikhte hi card, UPI, ya account access ko secure kijiye. Fir bank ke official app, helpline, ya branch channel se complaint kijiye aur reference number, screenshots, aur timing details save kariye.

Pehle 30 Minute ka Action Plan

  1. Confirm kijiye transaction aapka nahi hai.
  2. Card, UPI, ya account access ko lock ya block kijiye.
  3. Password ya app PIN badal dijiye agar risk ho.
  4. Official bank channel se complaint kijiye.
  5. Complaint number aur timing note kijiye.
  6. Alerts aur transaction details ke screenshots save kijiye.

Common Mistakes
