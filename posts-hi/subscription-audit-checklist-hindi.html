Subscription Audit Checklist: Silent Monthly Money Leaks kaise kaatein

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Har mahine kuch paise aise bhi nikal jate hain jo hume properly yaad bhi nahi rehte. OTT plans, app subscriptions, cloud storage, auto-renewed trials, aur forgotten memberships quietly budget ko weak kar dete hain. Ek short subscription audit se aap bina lifestyle overchange kiye paise bacha sakte hain.

Quick Answer

Mahine me ek baar recurring charges check karo, unhe real use ke hisaab se classify karo, aur low-value subscriptions cancel ya downgrade karo. Small monthly amounts milkar bada budget leak ban sakte hain.

Leak kahaan hota hai

  • free trial jo paid plan ban gaya
  • duplicate streaming ya music plans
  • old apps jo ab use nahi hote
  • annual renewals jo yaad nahi rehte
  • temporary need ke liye liye gaye premium plans

Audit Checklist

QuestionWhy it matters
Kya maine ise last 30 days me use kiya?Real use sabse important signal hai.
Kya main aaj bhi ise buy karta?Lazy renewals pakadne me help karta hai.
Kya cheaper option hai?Pura cancel kiye bina cost kam ho sakti hai.
Kya ye work ya health se linked hai?Kuch recurring costs genuinely useful hote hain.

Monthly Review Process

  1. Last 2 statements kholo.
  2. Recurring charges mark karo.
  3. Keep, downgrade, cancel, later-review buckets banao.
  4. Low-value renewals immediately cancel karo.
  5. Annual plans ki next renewal date note karo.

Common Mistakes

  1. Small charges ko ignore karna.
  2. Overlapping services ko ek saath chalne dena.
  3. Sirf UPI check karna aur card/app store renewals ignore karna.
