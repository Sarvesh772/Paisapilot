About PaisaPilot

PaisaPilot is an educational personal finance platform for Indian readers. We publish practical, example-driven guides in English and Hindi focused on everyday decisions, not hype.

Why This Website Exists

PaisaPilot exists to make money topics easier for ordinary Indian readers who want practical guidance without hype, jargon, or pressure selling. The goal is simple: help readers make calmer day-to-day money decisions with clearer frameworks in both English and Hindi.

What We Cover

  • Budgeting and cashflow systems
  • Credit cards, loans, and repayment discipline
  • Tax basics and salary understanding
  • Insurance and digital payment safety

Who We Serve

  • Students and first-job professionals
  • Salaried families managing monthly commitments
  • Readers who prefer clear Hindi + English guidance

Publisher Profile

Primary author: Sarvesh Kumar (Owner, PaisaPilot)

Location: Mirzapur, Uttar Pradesh, India

Expertise areas: budgeting systems, debt management, consumer finance basics, digital payment safety.

Publishing model: independently researched drafts, self-review, periodic updates, and correction tracking.

What Makes Our Content Different

  • We focus on everyday Indian money decisions, not abstract investing theory.
  • We try to add value through examples, comparisons, checklists, and action steps.
  • We publish separate English and Hindi content so readers can choose the clearest format.
  • We avoid miracle-income claims, shortcut promises, and fear-based financial advice.

How We Review Content

  1. Reader problem definition
  2. Practical framework and examples
  3. Independent self-review for clarity and consistency
  4. Publication with update date
  5. Periodic refresh when information changes

How Articles Are Maintained

Important pages are reviewed periodically for clarity, usefulness, internal links, and freshness. When a guide becomes too thin, outdated, or unclear, we revise the structure instead of only changing a few lines. We aim to keep articles genuinely useful for first-time readers.

Corrections Policy

If you find an error, email support@paisapilot.website with page URL and correction details. Valid corrections are updated quickly and reflected in revision cycle.
