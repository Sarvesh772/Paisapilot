UPI Safety Guide: 10 Rules to Prevent Fraud

Last updated: February 22, 2026

UPI is fast and convenient, but scam attempts are frequent. Following a fixed safety protocol can prevent most avoidable losses.

Quick Answer

Never share UPI PIN, never enter PIN to receive money, and verify payee details before every transaction.

10 Core Rules

  1. Never share UPI PIN with anyone.
  2. Do not enter PIN to receive funds.
  3. Verify recipient name before approving payment.
  4. Ignore unknown app-install links.
  5. Use phone and app lock.
  6. Enable transaction alerts.
  7. Check QR source carefully.
  8. Avoid unknown refund links.
  9. Keep apps updated.
  10. Report suspicious activity quickly.

Detailed Example

In a common scam, attacker sends a fake collect request saying "refund" or "reward". User enters PIN assuming money will come in, but payment actually goes out. Rule: PIN is only for outgoing money.

Action Checklist

  • Set UPI app daily limit.
  • Save official bank helpline numbers.
  • Keep screenshots of suspicious requests.
  • Inform family members about scam patterns.

If Fraud Happens

  • Call bank support immediately.
  • Disable UPI in app settings temporarily.
  • File cyber complaint using official portal.
  • Keep transaction IDs and timeline ready.

Final Takeaway

Pause before every payment. Ten seconds of verification can save major loss.
