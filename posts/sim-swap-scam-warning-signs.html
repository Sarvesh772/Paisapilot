SIM Swap Scam Warning Signs: How to Spot the Risk Before Account Loss

Last updated: April 29, 2026

A SIM swap scam can quietly take over the phone number linked to your banking and OTP flow. The damage grows fast when warning signs are missed for even a few hours, so early detection matters a lot.

Quick Answer

If your phone suddenly loses signal, OTPs stop arriving, and account alerts feel unusual at the same time, treat it as a serious warning. Secure banking access quickly and contact your telecom provider through official support channels.

Warning Signs

  1. sudden network loss without a clear reason
  2. OTP messages stop arriving
  3. unexpected account or app login alerts
  4. telecom-related messages you did not request
  5. UPI or bank access behaving strangely
  6. calls or texts failing unexpectedly

First Response

ActionWhy it matters
contact telecom official supportHelps confirm if the SIM status changed.
