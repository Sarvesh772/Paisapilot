Editorial Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

PaisaPilot is a personal finance education website. We aim to publish useful, practical, and accurate content for everyday money decisions. This page explains how we create and maintain content.

1) Content Purpose

Our content is written for educational and informational use. We explain concepts like budgeting, cards, loans, and digital safety in simple language. We do not provide personalized financial, legal, or tax advice.

2) Research and Drafting Process

  • Each article starts with a clear reader problem (for example, EMI planning or UPI fraud prevention).
  • We include practical steps, examples, and common mistakes to avoid.
  • We avoid unrealistic claims and guaranteed-return statements.

3) Quality and Accuracy Checks

  • We review clarity, factual consistency, and readability before publishing.
  • We cross-check calculations, ranges, and definitions used in examples.
  • We avoid thin pages and prefer structured guides with checklists, tables, and related resources.
  • When a page needs correction, we update it and refresh the date.

4) Updates and Freshness

Finance information can change over time. We periodically revisit high-traffic pages and update outdated sections, examples, and wording. Key guides are queued for periodic audit every quarter.

5) Corrections Policy

If you find an error, email us at support@paisapilot.website with page URL and correction details. Valid corrections are applied as quickly as possible.

6) Monetization and Independence

PaisaPilot may display ads and affiliate links in future. Monetization does not change our editorial standards. We aim for clear disclosures and reader-first recommendations.

7) AI Usage Disclosure

AI tools may assist drafting and formatting workflows. Final publishing decisions, structure, and fact checks are reviewed manually before release.

8) Contact

For editorial questions, corrections, and policy feedback: support@paisapilot.website.
